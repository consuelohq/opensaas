> Structured queries are for exact business facts.

## Use structured queries for

- people
- companies
- lists
- tasks
- calls
- statuses
- timestamps
- ownership
- permissions
- campaign ids
- counts
- filters
- exact relationships

## Rule

Exact business facts should come from the database/API, not vector search.
