> Consuelo OS turns a revenue workspace into an AI-operable business system.

## Definition

Consuelo OS is the operating layer where revenue teams and agents share the same data model, files, runbooks, sandbox, integrations, permissions, and artifacts.

```text
Consuelo OS turns a revenue workspace into an AI-operable business system.
```

## Product boundary

Consuelo OS includes a revenue workspace, business data model, steering files, small MCP interface, runbook manifest, Bun runbooks, permission metadata, GraphQL facades, workspace files and artifacts, sandbox execution, integration facades, and operator scripts.

It wraps the dialer, data model, files, runbooks, sandbox, and integrations into one agent-operable environment.

## Agent-swappable by design

Consuelo OS should work with ChatGPT, Claude, local agents, and future agents. The value is the operating environment; the agent can change while the context, runbooks, files, permissions, and data model stay in Consuelo OS.
