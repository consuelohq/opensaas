Consuelo uses AI across the platform to help your team sell more effectively.

## On-call coaching

Real-time whisper suggestions during live calls. AI listens to the conversation and surfaces objection handling tips, product details, and next-best-action prompts — only visible to the rep, not the prospect.

## Post-call analytics

Every call is automatically transcribed and scored. AI generates call summaries, identifies key moments, and flags coaching opportunities so managers don't have to listen to every recording.

## AI-managed queues

Intelligent queue prioritization based on lead scoring, response history, and cadence rules. The system automatically determines who to call next and when.

## Script mode

Upload call scripts and the AI follows along in real time, highlighting where the rep is in the script and suggesting the next talking point.
