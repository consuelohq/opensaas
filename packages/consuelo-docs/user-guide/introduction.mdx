Consuelo is a modern CRM platform designed specifically for sales-driven organizations. It combines powerful telephony capabilities with a full CRM, so you can make calls directly from your workspace without switching between apps.

## Who is Consuelo for?

Consuelo is designed for:

- **Insurance Agents** who need to manage client relationships and make outbound calls efficiently
- **Sales Teams** that require a unified platform for contacts, calling, and pipeline management
- **Small to Medium Businesses** looking for an affordable, customizable CRM solution

## Key Features

### Integrated Dialer

Make and receive calls directly within the CRM. The dialer supports:

- Click-to-call from any contact record
- Cold and warm call transfers
- Hold and mute controls
- DTMF tone entry
- Local presence for better answer rates

### Contact Management

- Import contacts from CSV
- Automatic phone number normalization
- Do Not Call (DNC) list management
- Contact queuing for parallel dialing

## Getting Started

1. Sign up at [crm.consuelohq.com](https://crm.consuelohq.com)
2. Import your contacts via CSV
3. Configure your caller ID settings
4. Start making calls

<Note>
  Consuelo requires Twilio for telephony. You'll need to configure your Twilio
  credentials in Settings.
</Note>
