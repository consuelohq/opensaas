Sales teams are stuck stitching together expensive tools that don't talk to each other. Consuelo replaces the patchwork with one connected platform — dialer, CRM, AI coaching, and automation — so your team can focus on selling.

## The problems we solve

### Dialers suck and cost too much

Most power dialers charge $100–300/seat and still feel like software from 2010. Consuelo gives you a full power dialer with multi-line dialing, local presence, and spam shield for $20/mo. Free CRM included after trial.

### Your CRM sits unused

If your team doesn't live in the CRM, the data rots. Consuelo is a CRM that manages itself — talk to it, tell it what to do, and it handles the rest. Contacts, notes, tasks, and follow-ups update automatically from your calls.

### Getting leads isn't the problem — contacting them is

You buy leads, but half never get called. Consuelo's power dialer increases contact rate and volume. AI-managed queues automatically prioritize who to call next based on lead age, response history, and campaign rules.

### Talk time is too short

When you do get someone on the phone, every second counts. On-call AI whispers help your reps handle objections in real time, increasing talk time and conversion rates. Post-call analytics show exactly what's working.

### No feedback loop after calls

Post-call analytics aren't getting done because it's manual. Consuelo automatically transcribes, scores, and summarizes every call — so managers see what's happening without listening to recordings.

### Team management is a mess

Lead disbursement lets management distribute thousands of leads across reps with one click. Chat SDK connects directly to Discord, Slack, and Teams — extending Consuelo's functionality to where your team already communicates.

### Leads go stale

Automation creates queues from old leads that didn't answer within a set period. Separate automations add contacts to follow-up queues one week, one month, and three months after a sale — reducing chargebacks and nurturing your pipeline.

### Sharing leads and CRM access is painful

Everything in the infrastructure is connected, so collaboration is no longer an issue. Reps see their leads, managers see everything, and nobody's stepping on each other's toes.
