title: Contacts Overview
description: Learn how to manage contacts in Consuelo.
---

The Contacts module is the foundation of your CRM. All customer information is stored here, including phone numbers for dialing.

## Accessing Contacts

Navigate to **Contacts** in the sidebar to view your contact list.

## Contact List View

The main contacts page shows all your contacts in a table format with key information:

- Name
- Phone number
- Email
- Company
- Last contact date

### Sorting and Filtering

- Click column headers to sort
- Use filters to narrow down your list
- Save filtered views for quick access

## Searching Contacts

### Quick Search

Press `Cmd+K` / `Ctrl+K` to open global search. Type a name, email, or phone number to find contacts instantly.

### Advanced Search

1. Click the filter icon above the contact list
2. Add criteria (name, company, tags, etc.)
3. Results update in real-time

## Contact Detail View

Click on any contact to view their full profile:

### Information Tab

- Name, phone, email
- Company and title
- Address
- Custom fields

### Activity Tab

- Call history
- Email history
- Meeting notes
- Task history

### Notes Tab

- Free-form notes
- Call recordings
- Attachments

## Click-to-Call

From any contact view:

1. Click the phone icon next to a phone number
2. The dialer opens with the number filled in
3. Click **Call** to initiate

## Managing Contacts

### Add New Contact

1. Click **Add Contact** in the top right
2. Fill in the contact information
3. Click **Save**

### Edit Contact

1. Open the contact detail view
2. Click **Edit** in the top right
3. Make changes
4. Click **Save**

### Delete Contact

1. Open the contact detail view
2. Click the three-dot menu
3. Select **Delete**
4. Confirm deletion

<Note>
  Deleting a contact is permanent. Consider archiving instead if you may need
  the data later.
</Note>
