## Installation

1. Visit the [Consuelo Dialer](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/consuelo-dialer) page on the Chrome Web Store
2. Click **Add to Chrome**
3. Confirm the permissions when prompted
4. The Consuelo icon appears in your browser toolbar

## Sign In

1. Click the Consuelo icon in your toolbar
2. Click **Sign In** — this opens your Consuelo workspace login
3. Enter your credentials (the same ones you use at app.consuelohq.com)
4. Once authenticated, the extension syncs your contacts and phone numbers

## Using the Side Panel

The side panel is the primary way to use the extension:

1. Click the Consuelo icon in your toolbar
2. Select **Open Side Panel**
3. The dialer, contacts, and queue tabs are available in the panel
4. The side panel stays open as you navigate between tabs

## Using the Popup

For quick actions, use the popup:

1. Click the Consuelo icon in your toolbar
2. The popup shows your recent contacts and a quick dial pad
3. Click a contact to call them, or type a number to dial

## Google Sheets Import

Import contacts directly from a Google Sheet:

1. Open a Google Sheet with contact data
2. Click the Consuelo icon and select **Import from Sheet**
3. Map your columns (name, phone, email) to Consuelo fields
4. Click **Import** to add contacts to your CRM

<Note>
  Google Sheets import requires the optional "Read your data on Google Sheets" permission. You can grant this when first using the feature.
</Note>
