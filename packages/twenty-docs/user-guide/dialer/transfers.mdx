---
title: Call Transfers
description: Learn how to transfer calls to other recipients.
---

Consuelo supports both cold and warm transfers, allowing you to redirect calls to colleagues or external numbers.

## Transfer Types

### Cold Transfer

The caller is transferred directly to the new recipient without any consultation. The original agent drops off the call immediately.

**When to use:**

- You need to quickly redirect a customer to the right person
- The recipient is available and ready to take the call

### Warm Transfer

The agent consults with the transfer recipient before completing the transfer. The customer is placed on hold during the consultation.

**When to use:**

- You need to brief the recipient on the call context
- You want to ensure the recipient is available before transferring

## Initiating a Transfer

### Cold Transfer

1. During an active call, click **Transfer**
2. Enter the recipient's phone number or search for a contact
3. Select **Cold Transfer**
4. Click **Transfer Now**
5. Your line disconnects and the parties are connected

### Warm Transfer

1. During an active call, click **Transfer**
2. Enter the recipient's phone number or search for a contact
3. Select **Warm Transfer**
4. Click **Consult & Transfer**
5. The customer is placed on hold
6. Speak with the recipient privately
7. Click **Complete Transfer** to connect them, or **Cancel** to return to the customer

## Transferring to a Contact

1. Click **Transfer** during a call
2. Start typing in the search field
3. Select the contact from the dropdown
4. Choose cold or warm transfer
5. Complete or cancel the transfer

## External Transfers

You can transfer to any phone number, not just contacts in your CRM:

1. Click **Transfer**
2. Select **External Number**
3. Enter the phone number
4. Complete the transfer

<Warning>
  International transfers may incur additional charges. Check your Twilio
  billing settings for rates.
</Warning>

## Transfer Best Practices

- **Warm transfers** are recommended for better customer experience
- Always inform the recipient of the caller's inquiry before completing a warm transfer
- Use **cold transfers** only when you're certain the recipient can handle the call immediately
