title: Making Your First Call
description: Learn how to make your first call using the Consuelo dialer.
---

## Prerequisites

Before making your first call, ensure you have:

- A Consuelo account
- Twilio credentials configured in Settings
- At least one contact in your database

## Step 1: Open the Dialer

Press `Cmd+D` (Mac) or `Ctrl+D` (Windows) to open the dialer sidebar. Alternatively, click the phone icon in the navigation bar.

## Step 2: Enter a Phone Number

There are three ways to enter a phone number:

1. **Use the dial pad** - Click number buttons on the on-screen keypad
2. **Type directly** - Enter the number using your keyboard
3. **Click-to-call** - Click the phone icon next to any contact in the contact list

## Step 3: Select Your Caller ID

Choose which phone number to display when making the call. If you have local presence enabled, Consuelo will automatically select the best number based on the recipient's area code.

## Step 4: Initiate the Call

Click the **Call** button or press `Enter` to start the call. The dialer will:

1. Connect you to the Twilio voice service
2. Dial the recipient's number
3. Bridge both parties into a conference

## During the Call

While on a call, you can:

- **Mute** yourself by clicking the mute button
- **Hold** the caller by clicking the hold button
- **Transfer** the call to another number
- **End** the call by clicking the hangup button

## Step 5: End the Call

Click the red **Hang Up** button to end the call. The call will be logged in the contact's activity history.

<Note>
  All calls are automatically recorded for quality assurance and coaching
  purposes.
</Note>
