> Use Consuelo in GoHighLevel two ways: run the embedded dialer inside GHL, or connect your GHL location to sync contacts and pipelines with your CRM.

Consuelo supports two separate GoHighLevel experiences. They solve different problems and they can be used together.

## Choose the right setup

| Option | Best for | Lives in | What it does |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Embedded Dialer | Reps who work inside GHL all day | GHL sidebar | Opens Consuelo in an iframe, handles click-to-call, secure sign-in, and in-call work |
| Integration | Admins connecting systems | Consuelo settings | Connects a GHL location over OAuth, imports contacts, syncs updates, and maps pipelines |

## When to use each mode

### Embedded dialer

Choose the embedded dialer when your team needs to stay inside GoHighLevel while calling. The widget loads at calls.consuelohq.com/calls/embedded, validates that it is running inside an iframe, and keeps the current GHL contact in context for click-to-call, queue actions, and call history.

### Integration

Choose the integration when you want data to move between GoHighLevel and Consuelo. The integration uses an OAuth connection, stores encrypted access tokens, imports GHL contacts into People records, receives webhook events, and lets you map GHL pipeline stages to your pipeline stages.

## Use both together

Many teams use both:

- reps launch the embedded dialer from the GHL sidebar
- admins connect the integration so contacts, pipelines, and logged outcomes stay aligned

<Note>
  The embedded dialer and the data integration are related, but they are not the same product surface. The embedded experience runs in GoHighLevel. The integration settings live in the main Consuelo app.
</Note>
