## The Problem with Traditional Sales Stacks

Most insurance agencies manage their sales operations across multiple disconnected platforms. A typical stack might include a dialer ($150/seat), a sales engagement platform ($150/seat), a CRM ($50/seat), coaching tools ($50/seat), and analytics ($50/seat). These costs add up quickly—for a 20-person team, that's $10,000+ monthly in software alone.

But the cost isn't the only problem. These tools often don't integrate well, creating data silos and manual work that slows your team down. Sales reps bounce between applications, losing valuable talk time.

## What Open-Source Sales Infrastructure Means for Insurance

The platform includes several key capabilities:

### Power Dialer with Local Presence

Insurance agents know that call answer rates drop significantly when prospects see out-of-area caller IDs. Consuelo's power dialer uses local presence technology, automatically dialing from area codes that match your prospect's location. This simple change can increase answer rates by 30-40%, helping your team reach more prospects in less time.

The dialer also supports parallel dialing—calling multiple prospects simultaneously and connecting your agent to the first person who answers. This technique can 3x your team's live conversation rate compared to manual dialing.

### AI Real-Time Call Coaching

One of the most powerful features is AI-driven real-time call coaching. As your agents speak with prospects, the AI analyzes the conversation and provides live suggestions:

- Objection handling prompts based on what the prospect just said
- Compliance reminders for regulated insurance discussions
- Talking points tailored to the product being discussed
- Tone coaching to help agents sound more confident and natural

This isn't post-call analysis—this is real-time guidance that helps your agents close more deals on the call.
