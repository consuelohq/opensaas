## transfers and conferencing

wavv has call transfers. consuelo has **conference-based transfers** that don't drop audio.

| Transfer Type | Wavv | Consuelo |
|---------------|------|----------|
| **Cold transfer** | ✅ | ✅ |
| **Warm transfer with private consultation** | ❌ | **✅** |
| **Conference mid-call** | ❌ | **✅** |
| **No audio drops during transfer** | ❌ | **✅** |

wavv uses traditional bridged transfers — tear down one connection, build another. that's why transfers fail and customers get dropped.

consuelo runs every call on a twilio conference. warm transfers let you put the caller on hold, speak privately with the recipient, then connect them. the caller never hears the handoff, never gets dropped, never has to repeat their story.

**keyboard shortcuts for transfers:**
- `t` = open transfer dialog
- `enter` = confirm
- `escape` = cancel

no mouse hunting during live calls.
