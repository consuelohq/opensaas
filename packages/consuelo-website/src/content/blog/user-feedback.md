## The numbers

| metric | industry average |
|---|---|
| time to first sale | 6-9 months |
| turnover in year 1 | 60-80% |
| cost to replace one agent | $15,000-25,000 |

most agencies lose agents **before they become productive.**

## Why onboarding takes so long

traditional onboarding looks like this:

1. **week 1-2** — classroom training, product knowledge
2. **month 1-3** — shadow experienced agents
3. **month 3-6** — solo calls with occasional coaching
4. **month 6+** — maybe productive, maybe gone

the bottleneck is **live coaching.** managers can only sit with one agent at a time.

## What faster onboarding looks like

| old way | new way |
|---|---|
| shadow calls for weeks | AI coaching from day one |
| 2 coaching sessions/month | coaching on every call |
| generic training scripts | personalized prompts |
| quarterly reviews | daily performance data |

> the difference isn't working harder. it's getting feedback faster.

## The AI coaching loop

every call becomes a training session:

- **before the call** — AI suggests talking points based on the prospect
- **during the call** — real-time prompts for objections and compliance
- **after the call** — instant scorecard with specific improvements

new agents get the equivalent of a **senior coach on every single call.**

## What this means in practice

| metric | traditional | with AI coaching |
|---|---|---|
| time to first appointment | 3-4 weeks | 3-5 days |
| calls before confidence | 500+ | 100-150 |
| ramp to full productivity | 6-9 months | 6-8 weeks |

## Getting started

1. set up consuelo with your team
2. configure coaching prompts for your product
3. new agents start making calls with AI support on day one

no lengthy training programs. no waiting for manager availability.
